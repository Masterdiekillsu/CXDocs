A 200lb 6' man holding a crude rusty shotgun walks with a heavy gait. His hair is greasy and wild, and should you get close enough, you smell that he clearly has not showered in days. He wears a gas mask over his face patched with duct tape, but the soft "cooh-pah" that it makes in time with his breathing clearly shows that it's functional.

Pirate Gunslinger

Cultist Initiate

A hooded figure wearing white robes seems to glide creepily along the ground. It's skin is a pale sickly green. As it waves its hands around, purple glowing nanite energy swirls in the air behind its gestures. Its movements are light, quick and deliberate. Despite the odds against it, it shows no signs of fear. Nor any emotion really at all.
