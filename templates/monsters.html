Pirate Breacher

A 200lb 6' man holding a crude rusty shotgun walks with a heavy gait. His hair is greasy and wild, and should you get close enough, you smell that he clearly has not showered in days. He wears a gas mask over his face patched with duct tape, but the soft "cooh-pah" that it makes in time with his breathing clearly shows that it's functional.
